Restaurant Data Analysis (2020–2025)

1. The number of restaurants in different boroughs of New York City shows an overall increasing trend (note: data for 2025 is incomplete as it only goes up to March).

2. A line chart provides a clearer view of the growth in restaurant numbers across different boroughs (note: data for 2025 is incomplete as it only goes up to March).

3. Multiple pie charts illustrate the changes in the distribution of restaurant counts across different years.

4. Chain restaurant data is identified using the DBA (Doing Business As) and CAMIS fields—restaurants with the same DBA name but multiple unique CAMIS identifiers are classified as chains. This chart shows the growth trend of chain restaurants from 2020 to 2024.

5. This stacked area chart shows the top six cuisines from 2020 to 2024, illustrating their trends in restaurant count and shifts in structural share within the overall dining landscape. It helps reveal which cuisines recovered more quickly and developed more steadily over time.
