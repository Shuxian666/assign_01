Green Classrooms: After-School Learning in East Harlem Community Gardens

Welcome

This platform maps and explores the relationship between community gardens and after-school programs in East Harlem. By visualizing spatial data on green spaces and youth educational facilities, it reveals opportunities to support children's growth, learning, and wellbeing through nature-based urban design.

Instructions

  • Click points on the map to see details.
  • Click anywhere to generate a 5-minute walk zone.
  • Use this as a resource for your design proposals.
